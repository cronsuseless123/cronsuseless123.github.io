HEARTBEAT

The heartbeat whispers in the night, A rhythm soft, yet pure and bright. It beats for you, it beats for me, A love that flows eternally. With every thrum, a secret told, In tender pulses, hearts unfold. A touch of love, so deep, so true, Each beat a vow I make to you. In sync, our hearts begin to dance, A timeless, gentle, sweet romance. The world may change, the years may fly, But in our hearts, love will not die. So hear the beat, the sound, the way, It echoes love, both night and day. A heartbeat shared in soft embrace, A love that time cannot erase.
