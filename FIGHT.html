FIGHT

In love, we fight, we tear, we mend, A storm that rages, yet will end. Each word we shout, each tear we cry, A test of hearts, beneath the sky. But in the clash, there’s truth we find, A deeper bond, a stronger mind. Through every struggle, through the pain, Our love remains, it will sustain. We fight, we fall, we rise again, A dance of joy, a touch of rain. For in each fight, we understand, Love’s power grows through hearts that stand. So let the storms around us roar, Our love will fight, and love once more. In every fight, in every tear, Love’s touch will calm and bring us near.
